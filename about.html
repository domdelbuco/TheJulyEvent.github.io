We are a film production company based in Gainesville Florida, who are committed to creating the first big local movie. Producer Trent Westwood and Director Brandon Friend have been dedicated to their creative journey for the past year. Together, we have built this company on minimal funds and the help of people who have endorsed our vision.


We have created an entire culture built on the success of hard work and fortitude from college students who have joined together in their journey to preeminence. They are currently working to finish their first film “The Gifford County Film Festival” written and directed by Brandon Friend. We plan on entering their film in the Sundance Film Festival this year.


Our passion and commitment to achieving this mission is for more than just recognition, we have the desire to change lives. Charity has always been at the heart of the organizations foundation. In our first project, we made a web series called “Haven’s Happening” which focused on giving back to the community while interviewing some of Gainesville’s most philanthropic individuals.
